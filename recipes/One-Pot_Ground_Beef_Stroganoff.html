One-Pot Ground Beef Stroganoff

HOME

Description

This one-pot ground beef stroganoff is creamy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 ½ tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • 1 ¼ cups beef stock
  • 1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. drain
  4. Combine cornstarch, salt, parsley, garlic powder, and onion powder in a small bowl.
  5. Pour cornstarch mixture, milk, beef stock, and egg noodles into the skillet and bring to a boil.
  6. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until noodles are tender, about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in sour cream and Parmesan cheese until just combined.
