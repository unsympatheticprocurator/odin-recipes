Ground Beef casserole

HOME

Description

A very easy and soothing dish

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ cup chopped yellow onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¼ cup chopped carrot
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (8 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 (8 ounce) package bow-tie pasta (farfalle)
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
  • ¼ pound shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the ground beef, salt, and black pepper to the hot skillet.
  3. Cook and stir the beef for 5 to 7 minutes until it is browned and crumbly.
  4. Drain and discard the excess grease from the skillet.
  5. Add the chopped onion, green bell pepper, carrot, and garlic to the beef.
  6. Cook and stir the beef and vegetable mixture for about 15 minutes until the onion is lightly browned.
  7. Mix in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, mushrooms, Worcestershire sauce, and brown sugar.
  8. Season the entire mixture with additional salt and black pepper.
  9. Simmer on low for approximately 45 minutes until the liquid has reduced and the flavors have blended.
  10. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  11. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  12. Cook the pasta in the boiling water for about 12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it is cooked through but still firm to the bite.
  13. Drain the pasta and transfer it into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
  14. Spoon the cream of mushroom soup evenly over the cooked pasta.
  15. Layer the beef and vegetable mixture on top of the soup and pasta.
  16. Sprinkle the shredded Cheddar cheese over the top of the casserole.
  17. Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes until the casserole is bubbling and the cheese has melted.
